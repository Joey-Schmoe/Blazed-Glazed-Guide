Intro

Glenwood Town


Go through the name choice and the starting scene like most games. After finishing that you will be at the end of a path.

Walk south a little bit and Luke (You don't know him yet.) will speak to you and teleport you into your house.

Go outside where your mom and dad will talk to you. Head over to Willow's lab and choose one of the 5 starters!
